Sound files needed for the game:

1. button_click.wav - Sound when a button is clicked
2. button_hover.wav - Sound when hovering over a button
3. x_move.wav - Sound when placing an X marker
4. o_move.wav - Sound when placing an O marker
5. win.wav - Sound when a player wins
6. draw.wav - Sound when the game ends in a draw
7. background_music.ogg - Background music during gameplay
8. menu_music.ogg - Music for the menu screen

These sound files should be free for commercial use. You can find such sound files on websites like:
- freesound.org
- opengameart.org
- soundbible.com
